Research Paper

TLDR: I conducted research at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) on the Magnetorheological (MR) damper location optimization for the mitigation of structural damage due to high-impact loads. MR dampers are smart control devices that can change from a free-flowing state to a semi-solid state in the presence of a magnetic field. I conducted impact drop tests to find the opimal location of these dampers on a bridge stucture. I collected displacement and acceleration data from my tests, and wrote MATLAB scripts to analyze the log files. I graphed the structural response reduction due to dampers to compare the effectiveness of the different combinations of dampers. I also wrote a research paper to be used as a foundation for journal publication, and I created a poster of my work to present to professors and graduate students.
